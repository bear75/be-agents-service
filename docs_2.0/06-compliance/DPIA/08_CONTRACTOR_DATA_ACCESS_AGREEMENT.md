## 1. Purpose of the Agreement

The Contractor is engaged by EirTech AB to support development, maintenance, optimization and delivery of the Caire platform during the Attendo pilot.

To ensure high quality and rapid iteration, the Contractor will receive controlled access to production data. This agreement governs the permitted use of that data and the Contractor's obligations.

---

## 2. Definitions

**"Personal Data"** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, processed within the Caire platform.

**"Production Data"** means live data stored and processed in production environments of Caire, including data originating from Attendo.

**"Confidential Information"** includes all data, code, architecture, documentation, business processes, decisions, visit information, optimization outputs, and all operational details of EirTech AB or its customers.

**"Contractor"** refers to any individual or company performing work for EirTech AB under contract, consultancy or subcontracting arrangement.

---

## 3. Permitted Purpose

The Contractor may access production systems and production data solely for the following purposes:

- Development, debugging and optimization of the Caire platform;
- Improving performance, stability, correctness, and quality of scheduling and optimization;
- Supporting EirTech AB during the Attendo pilot to ensure accurate, fast, and reliable delivery.

**No other use is permitted.**

---

## 4. Access Conditions

The Contractor agrees to:

- Access only the systems and data explicitly authorized by EirTech AB.
- Never copy, export, screenshot, record, or store production data outside the approved environment.
- Use only EirTech-approved tools, accounts, and security configurations.
- Never share access credentials with any other person.
- Immediately report any incident, breach, or suspected security event to EirTech AB's CEO.

EirTech AB may revoke access at any time without notice.

---

## 5. Restrictions

The Contractor must not:

- Process or use production data for any personal or commercial purpose unrelated to EirTech.
- Retain any personal data after the agreement ends.
- Build or train any AI model using exported production data.
- Store customer or user information outside secure EirTech systems.
- Disclose any information about Attendo, Caire, or EirTech AB to third parties.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

The Contractor acknowledges that all data obtained during the engagement is strictly confidential.

The Contractor agrees to maintain confidentiality:

- During the engagement, and
- For at least 5 years after the agreement ends, or longer if legally required.

The Contractor also agrees not to disclose any architectural or operational information about Caire, its optimization logic, data flows, concepts, or platform internals.

---

## 7. Sub-processors / Third Parties

The Contractor may not subcontract any work, grant access, or allow third parties to see or interact with production data without explicit written permission from EirTech AB.

---

## 8. Return or Deletion of Data

Upon termination of the engagement:

- All local caches, temporary files, logs, or artefacts containing any production data must be securely deleted.
- All access tokens, SSH keys, GitHub access, VPN accounts, or passwords must be returned or invalidated.
- A written confirmation of deletion must be provided to EirTech AB if requested.

---

## 9. Liability

Any breach of this agreement may cause serious harm to EirTech AB, Attendo, or data subjects.

The Contractor acknowledges that:

- Breaches may result in termination of contract;
- EirTech AB may seek compensation for damages;
- Unlawful disclosure of personal data may lead to GDPR penalties and personal liability.

---

## 10. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by Swedish law.  
Disputes shall be resolved in Stockholm District Court.
