## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how internal personnel at EirTech AB may access and process personal data within the Caire platform, including during pilots such as the Attendo pilot. It ensures compliance with GDPR Articles 5, 29, and 32, and is used during the Attendo pilot to support high-quality, iterative delivery.

---

## 2. Legal Basis

Under GDPR:

- All persons authorized to access personal data must be bound by confidentiality.
- Access must be restricted, purpose-limited, and controlled.
- The controller (EirTech AB) must document who has access and why.

This policy formalizes those obligations.

---

## 3. Scope

This policy applies to all internal personnel with system-level or operational access to:

- Caire production systems
- Scheduling optimization data
- Timefold datasets
- Logs, operations dashboards, and support tools
- Any personal data belonging to Attendo or other pilot customers

It applies regardless of employment status.

## 6. Permitted Use of Personal Data

Internal personnel may access production data strictly for these purposes:

- Development, debugging, and optimization of Caire
- Investigating issues, errors, or optimization failures
- Supporting the Attendo pilot with operational correctness
- Quality assurance and validation of scheduling outputs
- Ensuring compliance, security, and system integrity
- Reviewing domain correctness (home care expert role)

**Any other use is strictly prohibited.**

---

## 7. Confidentiality Obligations

All personnel covered by this policy agree to:

- Keep personal data and confidential information strictly confidential
- Not disclose or share any personal data outside EirTech AB
- Not export or store data locally unless explicitly approved
- Not train AI models or external tools using production data
- Maintain secure devices (passwords, MFA, encrypted storage)
- Report any incident immediately to the CEO

These obligations continue indefinitely, even after leaving the organisation or ending involvement with EirTech AB.

---

## 8. Access Control Requirements

Internal personnel must:

- Use only authorized accounts provided by EirTech AB
- Never share credentials or tokens
- Access only the minimum data required to perform assigned tasks
- Avoid storing or caching production data outside approved systems
- Ensure that all actions on production systems are logged and traceable
- Follow least-privilege access principles

EirTech AB may revoke access at any time.

---

## 9. No Employment Relationship Created

This policy does not:

- Establish an employment contract
- Establish salary obligations
- Change the legal relationship between founders, shareholders, and the company

It regulates data access and GDPR compliance only.

---

## 10. Term and Survival

The obligations in this policy remain in force:

- For as long as the individual has access to EirTech AB systems, and
- For at least 5 years after access ends,
- Or longer where legally required.
