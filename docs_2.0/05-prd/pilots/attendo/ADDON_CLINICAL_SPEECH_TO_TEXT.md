# Corti × CAIRE

## Building the AI Operating System for Home Care

### The Vision

Home care today runs on fragmented systems:

- Scheduling and routing are optimized in isolation
- Documentation is manual, delayed, and unstructured
- Clinical signals are buried in free text
- Operations, quality, and safety are disconnected

**CAIRE** is building the AI operating system for home-care logistics and planning.
**Corti** is the leading clinical AI layer for understanding what actually happened in care.

Together, we can deliver the **first end-to-end AI platform for home care**.

---

### Clear Role Split (Why This Works)

**CAIRE**

- Who does what, when, where
- Scheduling, routing, continuity
- Supply / demand balancing
- Financial and operational outcomes

**Corti**

- What happened during the visit
- Speech-to-text & structured notes
- Clinical reasoning & risk detection
- High-trust healthcare AI infrastructure

> **Corti understands care.
> CAIRE operationalizes it.**

### Core Joint Use Cases

#### 1. Zero-Admin Home Visits (Immediate ROI)

- Caregiver speaks after visit (mobile)
- Corti → structured note (Swedish, healthcare-grade)
- CAIRE:
  - stores note
  - updates care signals
  - feeds optimisation engine

**Outcome**

- 50–80% reduction in documentation time
- Higher data quality
- Real-time operational truth

---

#### 2. Avvikelser & Safety (SoL / HSL / Lex)

  - uteblivet besök
  - fall

  - arbetsmiljörisk
- CAIRE:
  - manages responsibility, follow-up, audit trail
  - links deviation to schedule causes

**Outcome**

- Systematic deviation handling
- Strong compliance story (IVO / municipality)
- Fewer repeat incidents

---

#### 3. Care-Plan → Optimisation Feedback Loop

- Corti extracts:
  - changed mobility
  - skill needs
  - visit duration
  - continuity preferences
- CAIRE:
  - updates constraints
  - immediately improves future schedules

**Outcome**

- Schedules always reflect reality
- Better continuity and fewer failures
- Deep competitive moat

---

#### 4. Risk & Proactive Care

- Corti identifies deterioration signals over time
- CAIRE escalates:
  - nurse visit
  - priority scheduling
  - follow-ups

**Outcome**

- Earlier interventions
- Fewer hospitalisations
- Safer home care

## **Document 2 — Attendo-Specific Corti Pilot Scope (Add-On)**

# CAIRE × ATTENDO

## Corti Integration Pilot — Add-On Scope

### Positioning

This Corti pilot is a **CAIRE platform add-on**, similar in structure to **Analytics & BI**.

- Built once for CAIRE
- Configured for Attendo’s organisation
- Optional, independent of core pilot scope
- No bespoke Attendo-only build

---

## Purpose

To evaluate how **clinical speech-to-text and structured understanding** can:

- Reduce caregiver administration
- Improve data quality
- Strengthen deviation (avvikelse) handling
- Feed real-world care signals into scheduling and optimisation

---

## Scope of the Pilot

### 1. Daily Visit Notes (High Frequency)

- Caregivers dictate notes after visits (mobile)
- Corti:
  - speech-to-text (Swedish)
  - structured daily note
- CAIRE:
  - stores note
  - links to visit & schedule
  - enables longitudinal analysis

**Goal:**
Reduce daily documentation time by **50–80%**

---

### 2. Avvikelser (SoL / HSL / Arbetsmiljö)

Corti classifies spoken input as **avvikelse** when relevant:

Examples:

- Uteblivet besök
- Fallolycka
- Medicin missad
- Otrygg arbetsmiljö

CAIRE:

- Owns classification, responsibility, follow-up
- Flags possible Lex Sarah / Lex Maria
- Links avvikelser to scheduling causes

**Goal:**
Systematic, auditable deviation handling without extra admin.

---

### 3. Journals (Low Frequency, High Importance)

- Important observations (illness, fall, deterioration)
- Corti structures the journal entry
- CAIRE:
  - flags client
  - triggers follow-up if needed

---

### 4. Care-Plan & Client Meetings

- Dictation during client onboarding or review
- Corti extracts:
  - skills
  - visit duration
  - time windows
  - preferred caregivers
- CAIRE updates:
  - movable visits
  - optimisation constraints

**Goal:**
Ensure schedules reflect real care needs, not outdated plans.

---
