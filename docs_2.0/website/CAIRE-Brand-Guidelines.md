    ## Brand Overview

    ### Mission Statement
    CAIRE is a Swedish platform that helps home care companies plan smarter, work simpler, and provide better care. By automating and optimizing administration, scheduling, and resource management, we free up time and energy – so you can focus on what truly matters: the people.

    ### Brand Positioning
    **"The Digital Infrastructure for Home Care"** - We see ourselves as an invisible but crucial support in the background that makes everything run smoothly. Just as rails enable trains to move forward, we help home care organizations stay on track – efficiently, safely, and sustainably.

    ### Our Role in Everyday Life
    We are the digital infrastructure for home care – an invisible but crucial support in the background that makes everything run smoothly. Precis som rälsen gör det möjligt för tåget att ta sig fram, hjälper vi hemtjänstorganisationer att hålla kursen – effektivt, tryggt och hållbart.

    ### Technology with Heart
    Our AI heart symbolizes what we believe in: that technology should enhance human work, not replace it. We build solutions with care – where intelligent automation meets human warmth.

    ### Core Values
    - **Innovation**: Pioneering AI-driven solutions for home care
    - **Care-Centric**: Technology that enhances human care, never replaces it
    - **Excellence**: Operational excellence through intelligent automation
    - **Sustainability**: Building systems that benefit all stakeholders
    - **Trust**: Reliable, secure, and transparent technology

    ## Design Philosophy

    ### Technology with Heart
    CAIRE's design philosophy centers on creating technology that enhances human work rather than replacing it. We build solutions with care – where intelligent automation meets human warmth. This is embodied in our core symbol: the AI Heart.

    ### Key Principles

    #### 1. **People-First Technology**
    - Focus on the people who matter most – patients, caregivers, and families
    - Technology that frees up time and energy for human connection
    - Warm, approachable design that feels supportive, not intimidating

    #### 2. **Invisible but Essential Support**
    - Like digital infrastructure that works seamlessly in the background
    - Clean, intuitive interfaces that don't get in the way
    - Sophisticated capabilities delivered with simplicity

    #### 3. **Swedish Values**
    - Trustworthy, reliable, and transparent technology
    - Sustainable solutions that benefit everyone
    - Balance between innovation and familiarity

    #### 4. **Empowering Workflows**
    - Smart automation that handles the tedious work
    - Intelligent scheduling that optimizes for human needs
    - Tools that make care teams feel more capable and confident

    ## Brand Symbolism

    ### The AI Heart: Core Symbol
    The AI Heart represents the fusion of artificial intelligence with human care - the essence of CAIRE's mission.

    - **Heart Shape**: Compassion, care, human connection
    - **Circuit Patterns**: Technology, intelligence, precision

    The CAIRE Flywheel represents our proprietary business model showing how AI-driven optimization creates self-reinforcing cycles of excellence.

    - **Four Stakeholders**: Balanced ecosystem approach
    - **Connecting Arrows**: Interdependence and mutual benefit
    - **Central AI Heart**: Technology as the enabling force

    ## AI Heart Concept

    ### Design Specifications

    #### Visual Elements
    - **Shape**: Anatomically-inspired heart silhouette
    - **Internal Pattern**: Subtle circuit board traces
    - **Glow Effect**: Soft luminescence suggesting energy and life
    - **Proportions**: Balanced, neither too technical nor too organic

    #### Color Treatment
    - **Primary**: CAIRE Green (#00FF7F)
    - **Secondary**: Deeper green for circuits (#00CC66)
    - **Accent**: Subtle white highlights for depth
    - **Background**: Transparent or dark for contrast

    #### Technical Requirements
    - **Scalability**: Vector-based for all sizes
    - **Simplicity**: Recognizable at small sizes
    - **Versatility**: Works on light and dark backgrounds
    - **Consistency**: Maintains proportions across applications

    ### Symbolic Meaning
    The AI Heart embodies CAIRE's unique position in the market:
    - **AI + Care = CAIRE**: The literal fusion of our name's components
    - **Technology with Purpose**: AI that serves human needs
    - **Life-Giving Technology**: Systems that energize and sustain care operations
    - **Emotional Intelligence**: Technology that understands human needs

## Logo Guidelines

### Primary Logo

The CAIRE wordmark with integrated AI Heart symbol.

#### Construction

- **Wordmark**: Clean, modern sans-serif typography
- **Symbol Integration**: AI Heart positioned as the 'i' dot or adjacent
- **Proportions**: Heart size should be 0.8x the cap height
- **Spacing**: Minimum clear space of 1x heart width around logo

#### Variations

1. **Full Logo**: Wordmark + AI Heart
2. **Symbol Only**: AI Heart for favicons, small applications
3. **Wordmark Only**: For text-heavy applications
4. **Monochrome**: Single color versions for special applications

### Usage Guidelines

- **Minimum Size**: 24px height for digital, 0.5" for print
- **Clear Space**: Equal to the height of the AI Heart on all sides
- **Backgrounds**: Ensure sufficient contrast
- **Modifications**: Never alter proportions, colors, or elements

## CAIRE Flywheel Design

### Design Principles

#### 1. **Product-Led Growth Model**

Following established flywheel design patterns:

- Circular arrangement of stakeholders
- Directional arrows showing flow
- Central driving force (AI Heart)
- Clear value propositions for each segment

#### 2. **Visual Hierarchy**

- **Primary**: Four stakeholder pillars
- **Secondary**: Connecting arrows and flow
- **Tertiary**: Metrics and supporting information
- **Core**: AI Heart as the central power source

#### 3. **Professional Aesthetics**

- Clean, geometric shapes
- Consistent spacing and proportions
- Professional color palette
- Clear, readable typography

### Technical Specifications

#### File Formats

- **SVG**: Primary format for web and scalability
- **PNG**: High-resolution for presentations and print

- **Hover States**: Subtle highlighting for web applications

## Typography

### Primary Typeface

**Inter** - Modern, clean, highly legible sans-serif

#### Usage

- **Headers**: Inter Bold (700)
- **Subheaders**: Inter SemiBold (600)
- **Body Text**: Inter Regular (400)
- **Captions**: Inter Medium (500)

### Hierarchy

- **H1**: 48px, Bold, CAIRE Green
- **H2**: 36px, SemiBold, White
- **H3**: 24px, SemiBold, White
- **Body**: 16px, Regular, Gray-300
- **Caption**: 14px, Medium, Gray-400

## Visual Elements

### Gradients

- **Primary**: Linear gradient from CAIRE Green to Deep CAIRE Green
- **Background**: Radial gradients for depth and dimension
- **Stakeholder**: Subtle gradients within stakeholder colors

### Shadows and Effects

- **Drop Shadows**: Subtle, 0-4px blur, 20% opacity
- **Glow Effects**: For AI Heart and interactive elements
- **Glassmorphism**: Subtle transparency and blur for modern feel

### Iconography

- **Style**: Outline icons with 2px stroke weight
- **Size**: 24px standard, scalable
- **Color**: CAIRE Green or white depending on background

## Application Guidelines

### Digital Applications

- **Website**: Primary brand expression
- **Mobile Apps**: Simplified versions for small screens
- **Social Media**: Adapted formats for each platform
- **Presentations**: Professional, high-contrast versions

### Print Applications

- **Business Cards**: Monochrome versions acceptable
- **Brochures**: Full color with proper color management
- **Signage**: High contrast for visibility
- **Merchandise**: Simplified versions for production constraints

### Brand Consistency

- **Color Accuracy**: Use provided hex codes
- **Proportions**: Maintain specified ratios
- **Spacing**: Respect minimum clear space requirements
- **Quality**: Use vector formats when possible

## Conclusion

The CAIRE brand represents the perfect fusion of advanced AI technology with human-centered care. Our visual identity reflects this through the AI Heart symbol, professional color palette, and thoughtful design principles that convey both innovation and trustworthiness.

Every application of the CAIRE brand should reinforce our position as the essential infrastructure that enables home care organizations to achieve operational excellence while maintaining their focus on quality care.
