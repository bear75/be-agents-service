## Slide 8 – Market: TAM – SAM – SOM

### TAM (Total Addressable Market)

Home care & assistance in Sweden: ~78B SEK total (public + private)

### SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)

Private home care (8B SEK) + private assistance (21B SEK) → ~29B SEK

### SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)

- Starting with private home care: 16,000 employees → 200 SEK × 16,000 × 12 ≈ 38M SEK/year
- Expansion to assistance (65,000 employees) → 200 SEK × 65,000 × 12 ≈ 156M SEK/year
